Base Case

$$
Fib(0)=\frac{\phi^0-\psi^0}{\sqrt{5}}=\frac{1-1}{\sqrt{5}}=0
$$

Base Case

$$
\begin{aligned}
Fib(1)=\frac{\phi^1-\psi^1}{\sqrt{5}}&=\frac{(1+\sqrt{5})-(1-\sqrt{5})}{2\cdot\sqrt{5}} \\
&=\frac{2\sqrt{5}}{2\sqrt{5}} \\
&=1
\end{aligned}
$$

Phi and Psi Identity by Quadratic Equation

$$
\begin{gather}
x^2-x-1=0 \\
x=\frac{1\pm\sqrt{1-4\cdot1\cdot-1}}{2\cdot1} \\
x=\frac{1\pm\sqrt{5}}{2} \\
\phi=\frac{1+\sqrt{5}}{2} \quad \psi=\frac{1-\sqrt{5}}{2} \\
\phi+1=\phi^2 \quad \psi+1=\psi^2
\end{gather}
$$

Induction Step

$$
\begin{aligned}
Fib(n+1)&=Fib(n)+Fib(n-1) \\
assume \\
Fib(n)&=\frac{\phi^n-\psi^n}{\sqrt{5}} \\
Fib(n-1)&=\frac{\phi^{n-1}-\psi^{n-1}}{\sqrt{5}} \\
Fib(n)+Fib(n-1)&=\frac{(\phi^n+\phi^{n-1})-(\psi^n+\psi^{n-1})}{\sqrt{5}} \\
&=\frac{\phi^{n-1}(\phi+1)-\psi^{n-1}(\psi+1)}{\sqrt{5}} \\
&=\frac{\phi^{n-1}\phi^2-\psi^{n-1}\psi^2}{\sqrt{5}} \\
Fib(n+1)&=\frac{\phi^{n+1}-\psi^{n+1}}{\sqrt{5}}
\end{aligned}
$$
